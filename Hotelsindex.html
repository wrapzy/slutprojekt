Hotels

In Tokyo most of the hotels are quite special in some way. Some of them are either just plain weird in most peoples eyes or cool and amazing.

Henn na hotel: Some hotels are even staffed by robots like Henn na hotel also known as The weird hotel. You also check in by talking to a robot either a dinosaur or a female android. With facial recognition technology you no longer have to carry around a room key card with you.

Hoshinoya Tokyo: In Hoshinoya every bedroom is in a traditional japanese style including low futon style beds, paper window bindings and sliding doors. The modern bathrooms are mostly impressive with large tiling, natural colors and subtle lighting. The hotel key feature is the hot spring bathing area on the top floor.
